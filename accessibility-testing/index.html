Name: Paulina Cosma
Website: Plainsboro Public Library
Date: October 2025

About

Hello, my name is Paulina Cosma, and I am a MLIS student at the University of Denver, as well as a current volunteer at this library. This term, I am taking a Web Content course, and we are learning about the importance of digital accessibility and effective strategies to make web content more inclusive for people with disabilities, including those who use assistive technologies. We were asked to perform accessibility testing on a local organizationâ€™s website to identify potential issues that might prevent people with disabilities or those using assistive devices from accessing the information easily. I chose to test your website.


Did you know?
As of July 15, 2024, the CDC reported that more than one in four adults in the United States has a disability, representing 28.7% of the total population (CDC, 2024).


This report aims to help The Plainsboro Public Library better align with Section 508 and the Americans with Disabilities Act Title II Regulations. The accessibility checklist is based on the W3C Easy Checks. Assessment tools like WAVE and ANDI, along with keyboard testing, were used. I hope this report proves helpful. Thank you for reviewing my recommendations.
